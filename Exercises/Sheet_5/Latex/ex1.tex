
\subsection*{(a)}

    Let $x,b \in \R^m, \alpha \in \R$ and $A \in R^{mxm}$. We want to find the gradient of :
    
    \[
    \psi = x^TAx+ b^Tx+\alpha    
    \]

    We have:

    \begin{align*}
        d\psi   &= d(x^TAx+b^Tx+\alpha)\\
                &= dx^TAx+db^Tx+d\alpha\\
                &= dx^TAx+db^Tx\\
                &= x^T(A+A^T)dx +db^Tx \\
                &= x^T(A+A^T)dx +d(b)^Tx+b^Tdx \\
                &= (x^T(A+A^T)+b^T)dx\\
    \end{align*}
So:

\[
D\psi= x^T(A+A^T)+b^T   
\]
